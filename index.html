That's us

We are a company founded in the pandemic year 2022. Together, we are five guys who know each other from the financial world. We have all worked in a large German cooperative bank and held management positions. Some of us have taken new paths, some have remained loyal to the bank.

We came up with the idea of founding a company because this employer keeps advertising with new awards. Most recently as the best employer in Germany. What a joke. Those who are affected by it, because they worked there or still work there, will tell a different story. So we came up with the idea of offering certificates ourselves. Our company Trust ET means "Trust EveryThing"

Besides the management theme, we have in common that we call ourselves "poker buddies" and meet once a year - often in the Black Forest - to take the mickey out of each other and, of course, to play poker. Without the (own) women we just let it rip. You know the movie Hangouver - exactly. And that's exactly what happens on this one weekend (every year)....
